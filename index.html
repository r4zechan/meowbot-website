🐱 About MeowBot

MeowBot is a Discord progression bot created to bring more fun, interaction and personality to online communities.

The idea behind MeowBot started from a simple question:

"What if a Discord bot could turn simple interactions into a real adventure?"

Instead of being only a utility bot, MeowBot focuses on creating a unique experience where users can progress, collect items, unlock rewards and build their own journey.

Inspired by progression, economy and collection systems, MeowBot combines:

  • 🐾 Interactive leveling systems
  • 🏆 Achievements and challenges
  • 🎒 Collectible items and cosmetics
  • ⭐ Community-driven progression
  • 🎲 Random events and rewards

The goal is simple:

Make every Discord server feel more alive.

MeowBot is currently in Public Beta. Development continues with new features, improvements and community ideas planned for the future.
