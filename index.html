🐱 About MeowBot

MeowBot is a Discord progression bot focused on making communities more interactive, rewarding and fun.

Every message you send can help you gain experience, unlock achievements, collect titles and discover surprises.

Instead of being just another utility bot, MeowBot transforms your server into a complete progression adventure.

  • 🐾 XP & Leveling System
  • 🏆 Achievement Collection
  • 🎖️ Unlockable Titles
  • 🎒 Cosmetics & Inventory
  • 🎲 Random Events
  • 💰 Economy System
