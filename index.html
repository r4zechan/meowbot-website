🐱 About MeowBot

MeowBot is a Discord progression bot created to make communities more interactive, fun and rewarding.

The idea behind MeowBot is simple:

"What if every message could become part of an adventure?"

Unlike traditional utility bots, MeowBot focuses on progression, collection and community interaction.

  • 🐾 Interactive Meow progression
  • 🏆 Achievements and challenges
  • 🎒 Collectibles and cosmetics
  • ⭐ Rewards and economy systems
  • 🎲 Random events

The mission:

Make every Discord server feel alive.

MeowBot is currently in Public Beta and constantly improving.
